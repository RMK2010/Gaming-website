Iconic Games & Consoles

Celebrating the Legends That Defined Gaming

Legendary Games

Pac-Man (1980)

The iconic yellow circle that became a global phenomenon. Pac-Man introduced character-driven gameplay and remains one of the most recognizable games in history.

Super Mario Bros (1985)

Nintendo's plumber hero revolutionized platform gaming. This game established many conventions still used today and made Mario a household name worldwide.

Minecraft (2011)

The best-selling game of all time. Minecraft's sandbox creativity has inspired millions of players to build, explore, and create in infinite worlds.

Fortnite (2017)

Epic Games' battle royale phenomenon that brought gaming to mainstream culture. Fortnite's free-to-play model and cross-platform play changed the industry.

The Legend of Zelda (1986)

Nintendo's epic adventure series introduced open-world exploration and puzzle-solving. Link's quests have captivated players for decades.

Tetris (1984)

The perfect puzzle game. Tetris's simple yet addictive gameplay has made it one of the most ported and played games across all platforms.

Iconic Consoles

PlayStation Series

From the original PlayStation (1994) to PS5 (2020), Sony's console line has defined generations with exclusive titles, powerful hardware, and innovative features.

Xbox Series

Microsoft entered gaming in 2001 with Xbox, revolutionizing online multiplayer. The Xbox Series X/S continues this legacy with cutting-edge technology.

Nintendo Switch (2017)

Nintendo's hybrid console that works as both a home and portable system. The Switch has sold over 130 million units, proving innovation in design.

Game Boy (1989)

Nintendo's portable gaming revolution. The Game Boy made gaming accessible anywhere, with Tetris becoming its killer app and selling millions.

Atari 2600 (1977)

The console that brought gaming home. The Atari 2600 popularized cartridge-based games and established the home console market.

Sega Genesis (1988)

Sega's 16-bit powerhouse that challenged Nintendo's dominance. Sonic the Hedgehog became its mascot and symbol of speed in gaming.
