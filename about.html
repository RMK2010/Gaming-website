About the Project

IB MYP Personal Project - The Evolution of Video Games

Project Purpose

This website was created as part of my IB MYP Personal Project to explore and document the evolution of video games through history, culture, and technology. The goal is to create an educational resource that explains how video games have transformed from simple arcade entertainment to a global cultural phenomenon.

Through this project, I aim to demonstrate how technology, creativity, and innovation have shaped the gaming industry over the past five decades, and how games continue to influence society today.

Learning Goal

My learning goal for this Personal Project is to:

  • Develop web development skills using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Create a fully functional, responsive, and accessible website
  • Research and synthesize information about video game history and culture
  • Design and implement a modern, user-friendly interface
  • Apply design principles and best practices in web development
